01About

I'm studying Electrical Engineering in the Honors Program at the University of Kansas — with a habit of picking up entrepreneurship, finance, and consulting work on the side.

I grew up in Overland Park, Kansas. Most of what I know about building things I learned by actually doing it — starting a promotional products company in high school, working through the Kiewit LEAD program on a clean water treatment facility, and shadowing founders through the Young Entrepreneurs Program.

Now I split my time between the KU Innovation Park, where I consult with bioscience and tech startups as a Business Analyst, and KU's School of Engineering. I like problems that sit between disciplines — where electrical, financial, and product thinking collide.
